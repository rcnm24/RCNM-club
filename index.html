About

Who are we? We are a college based Social youth organisation who works for the betterment and developement of the Community.

We have thirteen departments ranging from community service to entrepreneurship & professional developement to sports etc

We are a community of 300+ members and are based in a college with a footfall of 10,000+ students(75% attendance mandatory) ranging from age 16-21 age group. Over the year we conduct over 100+ independent events, and attend almost each and every Rotaract District 3141 event.

Now what Is Rotaract district 3141? -' It is a District Level Rotaract Organisation which ranges from Churchgate To Palghar which conduct Various District Level Events Throughout the year.

  • Promote peace
  • Fight disease
  • Provide clean water, sanitation and hygiene
  • Save mothers and children
  • Grow local economies
  • Support education
